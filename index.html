About the Brand

ABM Jewelry

ABM Jewelry is inspired by Monaco – elegance, sunshine, and a relaxed lifestyle. Our pieces are influenced by street style, catwalks, and modern art.

The brand story comes from a legacy in fine jewelry: years of working with gold, diamonds, and precious stones evolved into a contemporary vision focused on fashion silver jewelry.

Today, ABM combines craftsmanship with a playful, trend-forward spirit in every collection.
